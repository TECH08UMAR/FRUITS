IN THIS WEBSITE I WILL SHOW THE BENIFITS OF EATING THE FRUITS.

The nutrients in apples provide a range of health benefits. Apples are rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals, all of which benefit health. They also provide an array of antioxidants. These substances help neutralize free radicals.

Pineapple is packed full of fibre, vitamins and minerals and contains an enzyme called bromelain. Discover why that makes this tropical fruit so healthy...

Dragon fruit is low in calories but packed with essential vitamins and minerals. It also contains a substantial amount of dietary fiber.

Jackfruit is very good for you for many reasons. It is high in nutrients and antioxidants and may have a number of health benefits, including improved blood sugar control. You can easily incorporate jackfruit into your diet by eating it plain or in various dishes.
